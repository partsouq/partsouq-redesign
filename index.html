Policies & Terms

Payments & Orders

Auto Corner operates on Cash on Delivery only — no online payments are taken on the platform. Every order is reviewed by our team against the Mulkiya (vehicle registration) you upload at checkout before it is forwarded to the seller for dispatch, so fitment is checked before the part ships.

Cancellations

You can cancel an order yourself from My Account for as long as it's still "Pending Admin Review." Once an order has been approved and forwarded to the seller, cancellations are handled by contacting support directly.

Returns & Disputes

If a part doesn't match your order, don't accept delivery — file a report from My Account > Report an Issue and Auto Corner steps in to help resolve it directly with the seller.

Seller & Garage Partner Terms

Sellers and garages listed on Auto Corner are verified before their listings go live, and all listings are reviewed prior to publishing. See Become a Partner for full onboarding terms.

Privacy

This is a demo build — all data (accounts, orders, listings) is stored only in your own browser's local storage and is never sent to a server. Clearing your browser data will reset the demo.
