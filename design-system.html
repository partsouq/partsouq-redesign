PartSouq — New Color System

A light, low-glare palette modeled on the conventions Amazon, Facebook and eBay use for pages people read for a long time: a white or very-light-gray canvas, near-black (not pure-black) text, and just one or two restrained accent colors instead of a different hue on every tile. Dark navy is kept only for the footer — same as Amazon's dark footer strip — not spread across full content sections.

Why this replaces the old system

Before

Full-width dark navy blocks for the hero, "Buyer Protection" and "How It Works" sections force light text on dark backgrounds across most of the page — tiring over long sessions, and each trust icon used a different hue (orange, blue, purple, pink, green), which reads as decorative rather than trustworthy.

After

White/light-gray canvas with dark text throughout (Amazon/Facebook-style), dark navy reserved for the footer only. All trust icons now share one blue tone at different tints — calmer, more consistent, easier to scan for hours.
